"Tartaglia" is a for-profit, unofficial Genshin Impact fanzine contains Childe's artworks drawn solely by Zahravoca. The idea behind the artworks is based on 30-days drawing challenge made by Zahravoca herself as a token of gratitude that she had successfully got Childe in her account.

FAQ

What is a zine?
A zine is a compilation of creative works, usually artworks and writing. It’s similar to a book or a miniature magazine.What is this zine's theme?
This zine theme will be focused on Childe's life. Childe is one of Genshin Impact's characters.The zine will be divided by 3 stages and each stages will portray Childe as a cool and playful guy on different occassions:
- Never-Ending Performance - Who doesn't want to see Childe doing some cool poses that would make us blush? This stage will exclusively include all of his heroic poses!
- Teyvat - In the meantime, don't forget to follow Childe's daily activities when in Teyvat by looking on this stage!
- Alternative Universe - We like to daydream about this and that, and so does Childe! Want to see what is inside Childe's mind about his daydreams? Go check this stage!What are the zine specs?
- B5, perfect-bound, ~50 pages (these may be subject to change)
- SFW, PG-13
- For-profit, will be available both physically and digitally
- 1 artist only (Zahravoca, who handle everything — cover, pages, merchs)Will other character/ships be featured?
As the main focus of this zine is Childe, all pieces would feature him. Other characters and platonic interactions, however, may be happened.How will the zine team members be compensated?
Since the zine team members are only three of us, we already discussed before start making the zine and decided to compensate all of us with a full bundle of the zine, inclusive of the zine itself, all available merch items, stretch goals and shipping. Funds will mainly go towards production and shipping. The remaining funds will be distributed accordingly, depending on their contributions.This is a rough breakdown of how profits will be split among the team if we reach above 100 sales:
Sole/Lead Artist - 70%
Head Mod - 17.5%
Design Mod - 12.5%Will you open applications for the zine?
We decided not to open applications for any positions on this zine (contributors, writers, and mods), but this may change in the future. Any changes will be announced on our social media.Will you open group orders for this zine?
Absolutely yes! For more informations, please click on the link here.Where can I reach you if my questions aren't answered here?
You can contact us via Twitter DMs, Instagram DMs, or Tumblr asks!

Group Order

General Information

We are concerned that our shipping fees to many international countries are quite high. So we decided to open Group Order for Tartaglia: A Childe-centric Zine.What are the requirements to be Group Order Managers?
- Group Order experience preferred
- Minimum 5 physical orders (including bundles)
- Can pay using PayPal/Wise/Payoneer
- Must be 18 years old or olderQuestions we will ask to Group Order Managers
- What experience do you have with Group Orders/Shipping/Order Fulfillment?
- What is the approximate number of orders you are expecting?
- What is the maximum number of orders you can handle?
- Are you able to send payments using PayPal/Wise/Payoneer?
- What payment methods are you accepting from customers?How is the Group Order process?
- Group Order Manager takes orders and payments from customers
- Group Order Manager sends orders directly to Tartaglia
- Tartaglia sends Group Order Manager an invoice
- Tartaglia ship the bulk order to Group Order Manager
- Group Order Manager distributes orders within their region/countryWhat are the Group Order Managers’ responsibilites?
- Group Order Managers are responsible for all communication with the customers in their group order
- Must be informed about their local import laws
- Must maintain all order/shipping receipts from their orders
- Lost or damaged goods must be reported to the Group Order Manager firstDetermining and policies related to Shipping Costs
- Please DM to Tartaglia’s Twitter/Instagram (@30daystartaglia) or email (tartagliazine@outlook.com) about international shipping costs
- Shipping costs will vary depending on the number of orders, USD currency rates, and country destinations
- Group Order Managers are responsible for collecting domestic shipping costs from their customers

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Group Order?
A group order is a large order placed by one person on behalf of many customers.What is a Group Order Manager?
The Group Order Manager is a person which:1. takes orders and collects money from customers
2. places an order directly with Tartaglia’s shipping/production mod
3. receives the orders in bulk and distributes them to customersWhy do we provide a Group Order option?
- It will greatly save a lot of money on international shipping costs! For most countries, shipping will be a lot cheaper if purchasing on Group Order than purchasing individually.
- More payment options will be available! So far Tartaglia only accepts these payments: from stores (Storenvy, Gumroad) and international payments (PayPal, Wise. and Payoneer). International customers who do not use one of the payment options can buy and pay the zine from the Group Order manager using other payment methods.Do Group Order Managers have to be experienced?
Group Order experience is greatly preferred but not required depending on the manager. Any experience with shipping goods or order fulfilment is acceptable!What languages do the mods of Tartaglia speak?
English is our preferred language, but the mods can also speak:
- 日本 (Japanese)
- IndonesianWhere will the zine shipping come from?
We will ship all zines from Indonesia.What is the minimum order for Group Orders?
We require Group Orders to have a minimum of 5 orders. Group Order Managers are recommended to run an interest check to make sure they can meet this requirement.If you cannot meet this requirement, you can partner up with another Group Order manager in the same region and combine your orders. If you are close to meeting this requirement, please DM us on Twitter to discuss.How do I become a Group Order Manager for Tartaglia: A Childe-centric Fanzine?
You can contact us on Twitter/Instagram (@30daystartaglia) or on our email (tartagliazine@outlook.com)
